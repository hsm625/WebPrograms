Normal Probability Distribution

Normal Probability Distribution is a function that tells the probability
that an observation in some context will fall between any two real numbers.

Probability Interval

In statistics, a confidence interval is a type of interval estimate of
a population parameter and is used to indicate the reliability of an estimate.

T Distribution

T Distibution is a family of continuous probability distributions that arises
when estimating the mean of a normally distributed population in situations
where the sample size is small and population standard deviation is unknown.
